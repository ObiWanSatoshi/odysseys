The Itinerary

Five days of adventure, discovery, and celebration

Day 1 - Arrival & Grounding

Arrive via Pico or Faial with coastal transfer to base. Ocean swim, introductions, and an opening dinner in a private adega wine cellar. The evening unfolds into a fireside circle beneath the stars - no agenda, just connection.

Day 2 - The Deep Ocean

Full-day expedition to Princess Alice Bank, a mid-Atlantic seamount home to whale sharks, mobula rays, and whales. Charter vessel with scuba and snorkel options. Return at golden hour with whale and dolphin viewing along the route. Simple coastal dinner and quiet decompression.

Day 3 - The Volcano Ascent

Mount Pico ascent beginning in the afternoon, climbing into the evening to watch sunset from the highest point in Portugal. Summit camp above the clouds, waking to sunrise over the Atlantic. Shared fatigue and triumph - the core bonding moment.

Day 4 - Vineyards & The Dionysia

Morning descent, followed by rest and recovery at the lodge - cold plunge, sauna, stretch. Afternoon exploring UNESCO lava vineyards with micro-producer tastings. The final night is The Dionysia: music, masks, wine, and fire. An outdoor celebration that spills into the night.

Day 5 - Return

Big brunch and integration session as we share memories. A closing circle to reflect on what is carried forward. Departures.
